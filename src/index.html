What I do

ARCHITECTURAL CONCEPT & CREATION

I am a skilled professional experienced in BIM, Revit, ArchiCAD, architectural drafting, 3D modeling, rendering with Twinnmotion, and structural integration and coordination.

STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS AND DESIGN

I am a versatile professional specializing in structural modeling using Prota, Etabs, and Robot. I excel in assessing loads, analyzing structural members, designing steel, concrete, and timber structures to Eurocodes, and creating detailed drawings with Revit, AutoCAD, and Prota.

GEOTECHNICAL ENGINEERING

I specialize in Foundation Design and Retaining Structure Design. With expertise in modeling and analysis of geotechnical problems, I utilize Geo-slope software to ensure precise and reliable solutions.

TRANSPORTATION ENGINEERING

I have a strong command over Geometric design using Civil 3D. By leveraging this software, I create precise and efficient designs for roads, highways, and other civil engineering projects, ensuring optimal functionality and safety.
